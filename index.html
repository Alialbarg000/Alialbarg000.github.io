About Us

We Don't Just Fix It —
We Make It Look New

Sansoon Yacht Services has been fixing and restoring boats since 1999. Whether your boat has a crack, faded paint, or serious hull damage, we know exactly what it takes to bring it back to life.

We work on everything — jet skis, sailboats, motorboats, and superyachts up to 120 metres. Our founder, Zeyad Albarg, is also a certified marine surveyor, so you get expert eyes on your vessel from every angle.

Pre-Purchase Survey

Know What You're
Buying Before You Pay

Buying a boat is a big investment. The last thing you want is to find hidden damage after the deal is done. A proper survey gives you the full picture before you commit.

We inspect the boat from top to bottom, identify any damage or safety issues, and give you a clear written report. You can use it to negotiate a better price or simply know exactly what you are getting into.

Our surveyor has been working in the marine industry since 1999, covering everything from small recreational boats to 120-metre superyachts in both Canada and Europe.
